FAQS

Any optional service charge that you leave is a direct reward for the staff who made your experience special. We therefore ensure that all service charge goes to the staff in the restaurant where it was given. It is entirely your choice whether to pay the service charge. Please ask if you wish it to be removed.

We are pleased to welcome children in all of our restaurants, and offer an Enfants menu which can be viewed here. All of our restaurants provide highchairs, and most will also have baby changing facilities.

As much as we would love to welcome all dogs into our restaurants unfortunately only guide and assistance dogs are allowed. If the restaurant you are visiting has an outside area you are welcome to sit with your dog there.

Sometimes security settings can prevent the browser from accessing the final page of the reservations process. Try accessing the email on a different device or browser, perhaps try on a smartphone or a different computer if you have one. If you are still not able to book you can forward the email and your desired booking details to the restaurant you would like to book and they can complete this for you.

By signing up to our Westeros newsletters, you will receive updates of our seasonal menus and offers. If you have a question on an offer you have been sent from Westeros, please fill feedback form and we shall endeavour to get back to you in 48 working hours.

Our menu is either a 12 course or a 6 course tasting menu. The ingredients we use are sourced from the surrounding farms and gardens. Some of the courses will be small bites,others will be larger dishes. All will be cooked with Sri Lankan precision and techniques

If you advise us in advance of your visit we will always do our best to accomodate your specific dietary requirements. As far as allergies are concerned It is important to know that we do use nuts, gluten and other allergens in our kitchen so while we can ensure your dishes are free of these ingredients it is essential that you know that these ingredients are still used on the premises. If you need to inform us of any requirements, just give the restaurant a call on (+94) 081-2222225 before your visit to let us know.

Yes, we have a spacious car park so getting parked should be very straightforward.
